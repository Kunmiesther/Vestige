## Overview

Vestige is an institutional-grade AI reasoning terminal where market intelligence becomes a programmable onchain asset.

Instead of generating shallow trading signals, Vestige simulates a real investment committee using multiple specialized AI agents that debate market conditions, macro catalysts, volatility, liquidity, sentiment, and positioning before producing a final verdict.

Every reasoning trace inside Vestige can be monetized with USDC, unlocked through x402 payments, published on Arc, exported as institutional research, and verified through onchain transaction records.

Vestige transforms AI reasoning into a financial primitive.

## The Problem

Retail traders are flooded with shallow AI-generated market takes that carry zero accountability, no transparent reasoning, no verifiable payment layer, and no ownership of research.

Most AI trading products today generate generic outputs, hide their reasoning processes, cannot monetize intelligence directly, lack onchain verification, and produce no durable market records.

Vestige solves this by turning AI reasoning into a verifiable, monetizable, onchain intelligence asset.

Every trace has structured reasoning, agent disagreement, conviction scoring, risk analysis, catalyst tracking, payment gating, transaction verification, and archival publishing.

## Key Features

### x402 Paywalled Intelligence

Vestige monetizes reasoning traces directly with USDC. Users unlock premium traces through x402 payment requests before accessing institutional research outputs.

- Wallet-agnostic payments
- MetaMask, Rabby, and generic EVM wallet support
- USDC settlement
- Transaction verification
- Payment-gated intelligence

### Arc Publication Layer

Every premium reasoning trace can be published onto Arc, creating persistent and shareable market intelligence records. Published traces include the verdict, reasoning summary, committee consensus, catalyst tracking, conviction analysis, and transaction metadata.

### Wallet-Agnostic Infrastructure

Vestige supports multiple wallet flows including Circle Wallet, MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, and any injected EVM wallet. Circle Wallet is optional and not required for payment settlement.

### Institutional Trace Reports

Each completed trace generates a full research package:

- Executive summary
- Market thesis
- Bullish catalysts
- Bearish risks
- Volatility analysis
- Positioning analysis
- Committee disagreements
- Conviction scoring

Reports can be exported directly from the platform.

### Live Market Data

Vestige integrates live crypto market infrastructure for real-time intelligence generation.

Integrated sources: Birdeye, CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap
